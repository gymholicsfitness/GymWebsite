Our Journey

FROM PASSION TO
PURPOSE

Gymholics was founded in 2016 with a single dumbbell rack and a dream. What began as a small community fitness space in Artist Village, Maharashtra, has grown into the region's most respected training facility — home to over 2,500 dedicated members.

Our founder, a former national-level powerlifter, built Gymholics on one belief: that every person, regardless of background, deserves access to world-class fitness coaching and facilities.

Today, we house premium equipment, 15 certified trainers, and a community that shows up — every single day — to be better than yesterday.
